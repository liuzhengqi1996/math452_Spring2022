Question 1¶

Consider \(f(x,y)=e^{x^2+y^2}\) , compute the Hessian matrix and determine whether \(f(x,y)\) is a convex function.

Show answer

Answer: Hessian matrix is

\[\begin{split} e^{x^2+y^2} \begin{bmatrix} 4x^2+2&4xy\\ 4xy&4y^2+2 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Question 4¶

\[\begin{split} f\left( \begin{matrix} x\\ y \end{matrix} \right)=x^2+y^2 \end{split}\]

Given initial guess

\[\begin{split} \left( \begin{matrix} x^0\\ y^0 \end{matrix} \right) = \left( \begin{matrix} 1\\ 2 \end{matrix} \right), \eta=\frac14 \end{split}\]

, compute

two steps of the gradient  descent method for \(f(x,y)\):

\[\begin{split} \left( \begin{matrix} x^{k+1}\\ y^{k+1} \end{matrix} \right) = \left( \begin{matrix} x^k\\ y^k \end{matrix} \right) - \eta \nabla f\left( \begin{matrix} x^k\\ y^k \end{matrix} \right), k=0, 1. \end{split}\]
Show answer

Answer: \(\frac{1}{4},\frac{1}{2}\)
